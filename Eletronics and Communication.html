Eletronics and Communication Department


About the Department


The course Electronics & Communication was started in the year 1992 with Govt order No.ED 129 TPE 92 dated 12/8/1992 as an unaided course with a seat ratio 20:80 as Govt:Mgmt. It was started with a intake of 40 but from 2006-07 the intake has been increased to 120. Now the student strength of the department is 196.

Opportunities of the course


Electronics & Communication is a branch which is in great demand and students who complete E&C have good opportunities both in Hardware & Software. Application of Electronics in many industries is increasing in present days. Electronics has become a backbone to operate all heavy and light machines used for manufacturing and also use to control different machines.
Electronics is finding application in industries like Textile, Chemical & Automobile. Students get easy placement after finishing the course. Hubli- Dharwad has been identified as potential area for IT industries as far as infrastructure and Human Resources are concerned. There is a great demand for Hardware technicians and Network Engineers.
Students of Diploma E&C study subjects such as PC - Hardware and Servicing, Networking, VLSI Designing, PLC, Microcontroller, ARM7 Controller, MAT etc, which are of greater demand in industries.

Aim, Objective, Scope & strength of the Department

1_ Today there is great demand for qualified Electronics Engineers, to fulfil the present day demand in industries, field of Hardware & Networking and Embedded design.
2. The department of E&C has qualified faculty with specialization in Hardware , Networking & Software,
3. Students have good job opportunities through Campus interviews.
