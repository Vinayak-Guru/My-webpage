Computers Science Department


About the Department


Established in the year 1983 our department has made name and fame in the entire North Karnataka by excelling both in Academics and extra curricular activities. The Computer Science and Engg. Department is housed in a building constructed in an area of 300 sq. meter. With the Computer centre, hardware laboratory and staff room since the inception.
The department is approved by AICTE and got accredited in the year 2004 for 3 years. Ours is the first polytechnic in the entire Karnataka that is accredited.. The details of the approval are furnished herewith.
F-24-27/77 – T.5 dt 23/5/79 AICTE AUTO.
F-24-9 / 77 – T.5 DT. 26/12/79 Commercial Practice.
F-24 – 15/8 – III/RC(ABB)/93/26401/dt. 18/4/93 For all courses.
We are facilitated with well-trained, experienced and excellent almost Post Graduate faculties, who have enabled us to be specialized in various regular academic and corporate training programs.
