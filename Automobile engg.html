Automobile Department


About the Department


The department of automobile engineering was established in the year 1979.
The department Aided and recognized by Govt of Karnataka.
The department is approved by AICTE and Accredited by NBA New Delhi.
The department is headed by Sir A.A.Balikai and supporting staff members.
Every Year student is securing state level ranks and distinctions.
J.k. tyre, L&T, Behr India Ltd, TVS, TAFE & Gabriel India Ltd. Many other Companies recruited our students through campus Interview.
The department has well equipped labs and experienced staff member.
The department also runs Auto Consultancy Centre established in 1985 funded by MHRD New Delhi.
